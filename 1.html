Members

Red Velvet's members are Korean girl group formed by S.M. Entertainment. The group debuted on August 1, 2014, with their digital single "Happiness" and with four

members: Irene, Seulgi, Wendy and Joy. In March 2015 , Red Velvet added a fifth member, Yeri, to the group.
