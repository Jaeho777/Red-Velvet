Trophies

On March 11, 2015, former S.M. Rookies member Yeri was officially announced as part of the group.[24] On March 15, the group's first mini-album Ice Cream Cake was released.[25] The group promoted with double lead singles "Automatic " and "Ice Cream Cake" for which the music videos were released on March 14 and 15.[26] On March 19. the group held their album showcase on Ice Cream TV, a program streamed through Naver Music and hosted by Shinee's Minho.[27] On March 27, they won their first music show trophy on KBS's Music Bank. The album went on to become the best selling album by a girl group in South Korea for the first half of 2015 on the Hanteo Chart.[28] In August 2015, Red Velvet held their first U.S. performance during the annual KCON convention and music festival in Los Angeles, California.
